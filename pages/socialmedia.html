Social Media

Social Media can be a great place to share photos and stay up to date with your friends and family but it can also be equally as dangerous. Everything posted on social media is out there forever regardless if it's been deleted as it can always be recovered so remember, when its posted, it will be out there forever. On top of this, most employers will look at your social media pages when considering whether or not to hire you so make sure your social media page represents your moral values.



  • If you dont want someone to see it, dont post it

  • Remember that just because it is deleted doesn't mean it cant be recovered

  • Dont slander other people or bully others online

  • Dont post any inappropriate content on your social media

  • Make sure not to include any personal information such as where you are going
